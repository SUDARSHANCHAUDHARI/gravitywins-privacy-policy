# GravityWins Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 21, 2026
_Last updated: March 21, 2026_
**Version:** 1.0.0

GravityWins ("we," "our," or "us") is a health and fitness tracking app for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Information We Collect

**GravityWins does not collect any personal data. All data stays on your device.**

### Data Stored Locally
- Fitness activity logs, workout records, and health metrics are stored exclusively on your device using Room database.
- No account or sign-in is required.

## How We Use Your Information

- Display your fitness progress and activity history within the app.
- Track health and fitness metrics over time.

We do not transmit your data to any SudarshanTechLabs server. We do not sell or share your data.

## Storage and Retention

- All fitness data is stored exclusively on your device.
- Data is retained until you delete records or uninstall the app.

## Data Security

- Local data is protected by Android's built-in storage encryption.

## Permissions Used

- **ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION**: Required to detect and log physical activity automatically.
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** (Android 13+): Required to send workout reminders and goal alerts.
- **BODY_SENSORS**: Used optionally to read heart rate data from connected wearables.

## Your Rights and Controls

- Delete any fitness record at any time within the app.
- Revoke any permission in Android Settings → Apps → GravityWins → Permissions.

## Children's Privacy

GravityWins is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
